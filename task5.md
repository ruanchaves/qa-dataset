# Task 5: Share an AI Article

## Context
Anthropic recently published a blog post about measuring political bias in AI models.

## Objective
Write a short Slack-ready message you would post in the #research channel to introduce and share the article.

## Requirements
- **Format**: Slack message (ready to copy-paste)
- **Length**: Short and concise (2-4 sentences recommended)
- **Tone**: Professional yet engaging, appropriate for a research channel
- **Content should include**:
  - Brief introduction/context about the article
  - Why it's relevant or interesting to the research team
  - Link to the article (use placeholder if needed)
  - Optional: Key takeaway or question to spark discussion

## Deliverable
A single Slack message that effectively introduces the Anthropic blog post on measuring political bias in models to your research team.
